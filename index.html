About Me

Building Brands Through Data, Design & Direction.

I’m a passionate and results-driven digital marketer with over 3 years of hands-on experience in the fast-paced world of content creation and brand storytelling. With a strong foundation in short-form video production, graphic design and paid advertising. I help brands turn ideas into high-performing digital experiences that engage, convert, and leave a lasting impact.

What sets me apart is my ability to balance creativity with strategy. I don’t just create content, I craft narratives that align with business goals and audience behaviors. From attention grabbing video campaigns to visually striking designs, I ensure every piece of content is purposeful, on-brand, and optimized for performance.

I’ve had the opportunity to work with a diverse range of companies, including Hub Labs, Lish AI Labs, and Koupon Zetu, delivering marketing campaigns that have driven measurable success across both B2B and B2C markets. Whether it’s building brand awareness, increasing engagement, or driving sales, I bring a full-funnel approach that blends innovation with proven tactics.
